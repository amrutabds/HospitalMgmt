About our Service

The Adolescent Centre for Treatment (ACT) provides rehabilitative mental health treatment for teens aged 13 - 19 years. The facility consists of four apartment-style living areas with four bedrooms each. The anticipated length of stay is four months within the 24/7 component and four months follow-up with our Transition Team.
